# 📄 AI Contract & Legal Document Risk Analyzer

## Overview

The **AI Contract & Legal Document Risk Analyzer** is an intelligent document analysis platform designed to help organizations, legal professionals, and individuals quickly understand, evaluate, and compare contracts. The system leverages **Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), OCR, and Large Language Models (LLMs)** to automate legal document review, risk assessment, and contract intelligence.

Originally built using **Ollama-powered local LLMs** for offline AI processing and later enhanced with **Google Gemini API** for scalable cloud-based deployment and advanced AI capabilities.

---
